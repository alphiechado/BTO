Bukola Olajuwon, Ag. Head Consumer Electronics

Employee Spotlight: Oluwabukola Olajuwon Employee Spotlight: Oluwabukola Olajuwon

Employee Spotlight

Welcome to our employee spotlight series, where we ask questions about the lives of exceptional ipNX employees.
Today we have the pleasure of featuring Oluwabukola Olajuwon, Acting Head of the Consumer Electronics Division.
1. How long have you been working at ipNX?
15 years.
2. What do you enjoy most about working at ipNX?
The people are intelligent, inspiring, daring, innovative, dedicated, supportive, and fun at the same time. I also love the working environment created by the founders and the group executives which allows me to express creativity and innovations fully and freely.
3. What are some of the most valuable lessons you've learned while working at ipNX?
  • Stay focused, and work smart (innovate to ensure you meet and if possible, exceed your SLAs and targets)
  • Always improve yourself and your processes, you will surely be rewarded for it.
  • Don't be shy to ask questions when you're not clear on anything (tasks, objectives, or goals)
4. Looking back at your career at ipNX, what accomplishments are you most proud of?
  • Grooming young leaders and inspiring them
  • Automating the billing of corporate customers
  • Initiating the Corporate pay for our corporate customers during Covid
5. Beyond your specific role, what excites you most about the future of ipNX?
The organization, along with the industry it operates within, harbors significant untapped potential. Additionally, the forward-thinking vision of the organization and its exploration of diverse business opportunities signify our bold and innovative culture, as well as our pioneering spirit. These factors suggest that the company is at the outset of its journey, with promising and prosperous days ahead.
6. What are some key skills you have developed throughout your career that you find most valuable?
Communication, Negotiating, Leadership, Financial Management, Customer Focus, Adaptability and Problem-solving skills.
7. In your experience, what are the most important qualities for success in your role?
  • Leadership Skills: Ability to articulate the big picture to the team and inspire and motivate them to achieve their full potential while fostering a culture of collaboration, innovation, and excellence.
  • Strategic Thinking: Success in my role requires the ability to think strategically, anticipate market trends, and identify growth opportunities.
  • Financial Acumen: A deep understanding of financial principles and practices is essential for effective financial management and strategic decision-making.
  • Customer Focus: A customer-centric approach that prioritizes the needs and preferences of customers.
  • Problem-Solving Abilities: Success in my role often hinges on the ability to identify and solve complex financial challenges effectively.
  • Adaptability: The business landscape is constantly evolving, and success requires the ability to adapt to changing market conditions.
  • Constantly learning and unlearning
8. What advice would you give to someone looking to build a successful career within the technology industry?
  • Embrace Lifelong Learning: The technology industry evolves rapidly, so adopt a mindset of continuous learning and skill development. Take advantage of online courses, workshops, certifications, and industry events to expand your knowledge and stay ahead of the curve.
  • Cultivate Problem-Solving Skills: Cultivate strong problem-solving abilities, analytical thinking, and creativity to effectively tackle obstacles and drive meaningful results.
  • Focus on Adding Value: Strive to add value in every role you undertake, demonstrating your ability to create tangible value for your organization and it will give you an edge in a competitive job market.
  • Stay Agile and Adaptable: The technology landscape is constantly evolving, with new technologies, methodologies, and market trends. Be open to change.
  • Seek Feedback and Mentorship: Solicit feedback from colleagues, mentors, and supervisors to identify areas for improvement and opportunities for growth.
9. Tell us about your business role model of yours and what inspires you about this person.
Mr. Ejovi Aror, our GMD, serves as my business role model due to his remarkable resilience, unwavering can-do spirit, and propensity for dreaming big. He consistently challenges me to expand my vision beyond the confines of available resources, encouraging me to strive for ambitious goals and pursue excellence relentlessly.
10. Maintaining a healthy work-life balance is important. How do you achieve this balance in your own life?
  • I periodically evaluate what my priorities are.
  • I try to be clear on what my deliverables are at work.
  • Proper planning and time management are key skills I keep improving on, to help me prioritize, outsource, automate, and delegate effectively at work, at home, and in other engagements I have.
  • I reward myself for little wins with things that relax me, or I enjoy doing.
  • I retrospect, meditate, and consciously try to be self-aware.
  • I am never shy to seek help and support where needed.
  • I practice deep breathing exercises to reduce stress.
  • I have become more intentional; I am still a work in progress in maintaining a healthy work-life balance.
11. What are some of your outside-of-work hobbies and interests?
Acting, Swimming, music, traveling, exploring food.
12. Can you share a fun fact or hidden talent that most people do not know about you?
I have a passion for acting, whether it's performing in stage plays or participating in movie productions.
13. If you could have any superpower, what would it be and why?
I think I'd choose healing hands lol. I don't like seeing people in pain. Majorly because I would have loved to heal my family and friends who died from illness and save other people from dying.
