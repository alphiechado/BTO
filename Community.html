MVNO Nigeria 2025 Conference

Participating in discussions about the future of Mobile Virtual Network Operators in Nigeria.

A digital infrastructure platform committed to advancing Africa's compute ecosystem convened an exclusive breakfast session with senior executives, MVNO operators, investors, and policy stakeholders to explore the strategic and operational imperatives shaping Nigeria's MVNO future.

A key highlight was the involvement of our Director of Strategic Business Initiatives, Olusola Teniola, who spoke as a panelist during the session "Unlocking Growth, Competition & Connectivity" held at the Radisson Blu Anchorage Hotel, Victoria Island, Lagos on Wednesday, May 21st, 2025.
